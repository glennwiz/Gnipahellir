# Gnipahellir 3 — Playtester Guide

Hey! You're the first real playtester for this game. That makes you the most
important person in the room right now.

**Your job is NOT to be nice about it.** If something is boring, confusing,
or broken, saying so is exactly what helps. "It was fine" teaches us nothing.
"I died to lava and had no idea why" is gold.

One warning before you start: **this is a roguelike — when you die, your
save is wiped and you start over.** That's on purpose. Dying is part of
the test.

---

## What the game is

You're a fragile little hero in a Norse underworld. You mine, craft tools,
build machines, climb into the sky, and dig ever deeper — until you meet
**Garm**, the hell hound. Kill him, take the Hell Key, win.

---

## Controls

| Key | What it does |
|---|---|
| **A / D** (or arrows) | Move |
| **W / ↑ / Space** | Jump |
| **Left-click (hold)** | Mine — point roughly toward a tile and hold |
| **Left-click on enemy** | Attack (you need a weapon equipped first) |
| **Right-click** | Place the selected item in the world |
| **TAB** | Inventory (pick a slot with click or keys **1–8**) |
| **C** | Crafting window (green rows = you can afford them) |
| **E** | Interact — enter portals, use altars, open machines |
| **Q** | Drop the selected stack on the ground ahead of you |
| **ESC** | Close windows / pause menu |
| **F11** | Fullscreen |

Extra tips:
- Windows (inventory, crafting, smelter) can be **dragged** by their top bar.
- In the open inventory, **right-click** an item to equip it (weapon/armor
  slots above the bag); right-click an equipped item to take it off.
- The **smelter** has its own window: drag ore **and wood** into it, wait,
  then click the tray to collect your metal bars.

## Part 1 — Play blind (about 20 minutes)

Just play. No goals, no help, no one talking over your shoulder.
Try to figure the game out on your own — that's the whole point of this part.

When the 20 minutes are up (or you die), answer these:

1. What did you think you were supposed to do?
2. What was the FIRST moment you felt confused?
3. What was the first moment you felt "ooh, cool"? (If never — say so!)
4. Did anything kill you? Did it feel fair?
5. Rate the first 20 minutes: 1 (bored) – 5 (didn't want to stop)

---

## Part 2 — Missions

Now try to do these on purpose. For each one, note whether you managed it,
how long it took, and whether anything felt annoying or unclear.

- [ ] **Mission 1:** Chop a tree and mine some stone.
- [ ] **Mission 2:** Craft a **Crafting Bench**, place it, and craft a
      **Sword** at it. Equip the sword.
- [ ] **Mission 3:** Find the cave (hole in the grass near the middle of the
      map) and go down. Find the **Blueprint** hidden in the cave.
- [ ] **Mission 4:** Build a **Smelter**, feed it ore + wood, and collect a
      metal bar from the tray.
- [ ] **Mission 5:** Find the **sky gate** (far west on the surface) and mine
      some Cloud Ore up there. Careful — falling drops you back down.
- [ ] **Mission 6:** Place a **Sky Altar**, stand next to it, press **E**,
      and complete the ritual (the popup tells you what it costs).
- [ ] **Mission 7 (hard):** Get through the deep caves, wake **Garm**, and
      kill him. Grab the Hell Key. Win the game.

It's totally fine if you don't finish them all. *Where you got stuck is the
most useful information in this whole document.*

---

## Part 3 — The big questions

Answer honestly. Short answers are fine, brutal answers are better.

1. Fun overall, 1–10?
2. What was the most fun single moment?
3. What was the most annoying thing?
4. Was anything too hard? Too easy?
5. Did the mining feel good? (clicking, digging tunnels)
6. Did fighting the wolf-builders in the caves feel good?
7. If you could ADD one thing to this game, what would it be?
8. If you could REMOVE one thing, what would it be?
9. Would you play this again tomorrow without being asked? Why / why not?

---

## Bug reports

If something looks broken (stuck in a wall, item vanished, crash, weird
graphics), write it down like this:

> **What happened:**
> **What I was doing right before:**
> **Can you make it happen again?** yes / no / didn't try

Known things that are NOT bugs (don't report these):
- The Iron Bucket can't scoop lava yet.
- Potions exist but can't be obtained or used yet.
- Death (and winning!) wipes the save. On purpose.
